Carroll Gardens House is nestled in the heart of Carroll Gardens, one of Brooklyn’s most charming brownstone neighborhoods.

The apartment is one full floor of a brownstone: approximately 1000 square feet, with full kitchen, private bath, living/dining area, two bedrooms (each with a queen-size bed), small den and private outdoor deck. It's the ideal home base whether your visit is for business or pleasure.

Enjoy the many amenities this wonderful setting has to offer. There are over 100 restaurants and cafes within walking distance, dozens of family-owned shops and local boutiques specializing in everything from children's clothing to housewares, and the beautiful Carroll Park directly across the street with 2 playgrounds, a softball field, a bocce court, 5 basketball courts, and a sprinkler in the summer. Our location just half block from the subway couldn't be more convenient as a home base from which to explore New York's most popular borough.
